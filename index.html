About me

A technology leader who speaks the language of the boardroom

I am a telecom and digital-transformation executive with more than 25 years building and modernizing mobile networks from greenfield launch to nationwide 5G. As a Chief Technology & Information Officer, I translate business plans into technology roadmaps, budgets and projects — balancing coverage, capacity, quality and cost to hit commercial targets.

My work sits at the intersection of engineering, finance and strategy: preparing CAPEX/OPEX budgets, negotiating multi-million-dollar carrier and IRU contracts, running revenue and profitability analysis with the CEO and CMO, and leading network swaps, fiber rollout, mobile money and digital services. I have launched and operated networks across Afghanistan, Sudan, Syria, Liberia, Benin, Ghana and Lebanon.
