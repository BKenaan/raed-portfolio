A premium, minimal corporate card themed around telecom, digital connectivity and
consulting — designed as a visual sibling of **raedkenaan.com** (same palette,

## Print specifications
- **Final card size (trim):** 3.5 × 2 in (88.9 × 50.8 mm) — standard US business card
- **With bleed:** 3.75 × 2.25 in (0.125 in / 3 mm bleed on every edge)
- **Safe zone:** all text & the QR sit ≥ 0.15 in inside the trim — nothing important gets cut
- **Resolution:** 300 DPI (print standard)
- **Color:** RGB. Most modern printers (MOO, Vistaprint, local shops) accept RGB and
  convert to CMYK. If your printer requires CMYK, ask them to convert, or tell me and
  I can advise. Expect very slight color shift on the blues when converted to CMYK.
- **Recommended stock:** 350–400 gsm, **matte or soft-touch** laminate (best for the
  dark navy — avoids fingerprints and looks premium). Spot-UV on the monogram/QR is a
  nice optional upgrade.

## Layout

- `RK` gradient monogram — top-left
- **Raed Kenaan** — large headline
- *Chief Technology & Information Officer* — subtitle
- Short gradient accent rule
- Footer micro-tagline: TELECOM · DIGITAL TRANSFORMATION · INVESTMENT · CONSULTING
- Subtle network-node pattern + soft blue glow on the right

**Back (contact + action):**
- Header: small `RK` monogram + name + title
- Four contact rows with gradient icon tiles:
  📞 +971 58 815 2961 · ✉ kanaan5g@gmail.com · 🌐 raedkenaan.com · in linkedin.com/in/raed-kanaan
- QR code (→ https://raedkenaan.com) on a white rounded tile, captioned "SCAN FOR PORTFOLIO"
- QR verified scannable (decodes to the live site)

## Typography
- **Sora** (ExtraBold / Bold) — name & monogram. Modern geometric display face.
- **Inter** (SemiBold / Medium) — title, contact details, labels. Highly legible.
- Both are free (Google Fonts / SIL OFL) and match the website exactly.

## Regenerating / editing
Art is generated by `.build/card_gen.py` (Python + Pillow). To tweak text, colors or
layout, edit the constants/sections at the top of that script and re-run:
```bash
cd business-card/.build && python card_gen.py
```
